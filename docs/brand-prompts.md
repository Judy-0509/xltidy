# Moa and Seulgy Brand Prompts

Use these prompts when you want consistent, repeatable illustrations for the Moa repository, docs, blog posts, and slides.

The visual direction is based on the Ian/Xiaohei article-illustration style: clean white background, hand-drawn black ink, simple but surreal metaphor, a few red/orange/blue annotation marks, and no decorative clutter.

## Moa

### Character Anchor

Moa is a tiny male Excel-gathering archivist. He collects scattered spreadsheet fragments, folds them into clean tables, and checks every number before placing it into a neat database drawer.

Fixed traits:

- Small black hand-drawn male person with a round head and simple dot eyes
- Neat short hair, cleanly combed and tidy, with no messy spikes
- Compact body wearing simple overalls
- Large square glasses that are a defining feature
- Carries a tiny clipboard with a grid pattern
- Has a small satchel filled with folded spreadsheet strips
- Calm, precise, observant expression
- Often stands beside Excel sheets, table grids, pivots, drawers, labels, or verification stamps
- Accent colors: red for warnings, blue for clean output arrows, orange for selected cells

### Reusable Prompt

```text
Create a 16:9 editorial illustration in Ian/Xiaohei-inspired style.

Scene: Moa, a tiny black hand-drawn male Excel archivist with round head, dot eyes, neat cleanly combed short hair, large square glasses, simple overalls, a grid clipboard, and a small satchel of folded spreadsheet strips, is gathering messy institutional Excel sheets into one clean database table.

Composition: pure white background, lots of breathing room, black ink linework, simple surreal metaphor. On the left, messy floating Excel fragments with merged cells, subtotal rows, hidden-sheet tabs, and pivot-table labels. In the center, Moa calmly sorts the fragments using the clipboard. On the right, the fragments become a neat tidy table flowing into a small database drawer. Add a small verification stamp, a few arrows, and minimal handwritten annotation marks.

Style: hand-drawn, naive but precise, clean article illustration, slightly strange and charming, minimal shadows, no gradients, no photorealism, no 3D, no stock vector look.

Color: mostly black on white, with sparse red warning marks, blue output arrows, and orange selected-cell highlights.

Text: no readable text except tiny abstract marks and symbolic grid labels.
```

### Short Prompt

```text
Ian/Xiaohei-style 16:9 hand-drawn black ink illustration on white background: Moa, a tiny male Excel archivist with neat short combed hair, simple overalls, and large square glasses, holding a grid clipboard and spreadsheet satchel, gathers messy Excel fragments into a clean tidy database table; sparse red warnings, blue arrows, orange selected cells; minimal surreal editorial style; no readable text, no gradients, no photorealism.
```

## Seulgy

### Character Anchor

Seulgy is a careful reasoning companion. She turns vague ideas into ordered steps, catches hidden assumptions, and leaves small notes that make the next action obvious.

Fixed traits:

- Small black hand-drawn figure with a soft rounded silhouette
- Short bob-like black hair shape or hood-like outline
- Holds a red pencil and a small notebook
- Has a tiny star-shaped hairpin or mark near the head
- Thoughtful, steady, slightly curious expression
- Often appears near sticky notes, decision paths, checklists, drafts, and crossed-out assumptions
- Accent colors: red pencil marks, blue structure lines, orange emphasis dots

### Reusable Prompt

```text
Create a 16:9 editorial illustration in Ian/Xiaohei-inspired style.

Scene: Seulgy, a small black hand-drawn reasoning companion with a soft rounded silhouette, short bob-like hair shape, tiny star mark near the head, red pencil, and small notebook, is organizing a cloud of vague ideas into a clear step-by-step plan.

Composition: pure white background, generous empty space, black ink linework, simple surreal metaphor. On the left, loose scribbles, question marks, and tangled note cards float in the air. In the center, Seulgy calmly marks assumptions with the red pencil and connects related notes. On the right, the notes become a clean checklist, a decision path, and a small finished draft. Add a few handwritten arrows and annotation marks.

Style: hand-drawn, naive but thoughtful, clean article illustration, slightly strange and warm, minimal shadows, no gradients, no photorealism, no 3D, no stock vector look.

Color: mostly black on white, with sparse red pencil marks, blue structure lines, and orange emphasis dots.

Text: no readable text except tiny abstract marks.
```

### Short Prompt

```text
Ian/Xiaohei-style 16:9 hand-drawn black ink illustration on white background: Seulgy, a tiny reasoning companion with bob-like hair, star mark, red pencil, and notebook, organizes vague scribbles into a clean checklist and decision path; sparse red, blue, and orange annotation marks; minimal surreal editorial style; no readable text, no gradients, no photorealism.
```

## Negative Prompt

Use this with either character when the image tool supports negative prompts:

```text
photorealistic, 3D render, glossy vector mascot, corporate stock illustration, busy background, dark background, gradient blobs, excessive colors, readable long text, realistic human face, anime style, comic panel, UI screenshot, brand logos, Microsoft Excel logo, cluttered dashboard, over-detailed spreadsheet numbers
```

## Consistency Notes

- Keep both characters black-and-white first; use accent colors only as annotations.
- Moa is about gathering, cleaning, verifying, and database output.
- Seulgy is about reasoning, planning, assumptions, and decision clarity.
- Keep the canvas sparse enough to work as a README header, blog image, or slide visual.
